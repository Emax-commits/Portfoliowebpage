What I Do?

Frontend Development

Front-end development focuses on creating the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) of a website or application, essentially everything the user sees and interacts with. This involves using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build the structure, style, and interactivity of the interface.

UI\UX Design

UI/UX design encompasses both the visual appearance (UI) and the overall user experience (UX) of a product, primarily digital interfaces like websites and apps. UI focuses on the aesthetic and interactive elements users see, while UX prioritizes how users interact with the interface and their overall experience.

Video Editting

Video editing is the process of manipulating digital footage to create a coherent and compelling video. It involves cutting, arranging, and organizing clips, adding transitions, and incorporating other elements like sound and visual effects to craft a finished product.

Tech. Hackathons

Hackathons are intensive, collaborative coding events where individuals or teams work together to develop software solutions within a limited timeframe, often a day or a few days. The goal is typically to create a working prototype or a new product by the end of the event. Hackathons can be educational, competitive, or a platform for startups, and they often involve pitching ideas, creating prototypes, and demonstrating projects to judges.
